Short bio

Heleno de Souza Campos Junior (Heleno Campos) is currently a Substitute Teacher and a Postdoc at the Computing Institute of Universidade Federal Fluminense (UFF). He holds a D.Sc. (2025) degree in Computing from Universidade Federal Fluminense (UFF). He also holds a M.Sc. (2018) degree in Computer Science (Software Engineering) from Universidade Federal de Juiz de Fora (UFJF), and a B.Sc. (2016) degree in Information Systems from IF Sudeste MG. He held a CAPES scholarship for a sandwich program during the B.Sc. in 2013/2014, where he attended the University of Pisa, Italy. He also held a CAPES scholarship during his Master’s and Doctorate’s. His research interests include software testing and configuration management.
