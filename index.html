Short bio

Heleno de Souza Campos Junior (Heleno Campos) is a full-time Doctorate student at the Computing Institute of Universidade Federal Fluminense (UFF), currently holding a CAPES scholarship. He holds a M.S. (2018) degree in Computer Science (Software Engineering) from Universidade Federal de Juiz de Fora (UFJF), and a B.S. (2016) degree in Information Systems from IF Sudeste MG. He held a CAPES scholarship for a sandwich program during the B.S. in 2013/2014, where he stayed in University of Pisa, Italy. He also held a CAPES scholarship during his Master’s. His research interests include software testing and configuration management.
